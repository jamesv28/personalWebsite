About James Volmert

My name is James Volmert. I am a full-stack developer who also does web design in my free time. I have also played with SEO at times, and love analytics. I am based right out of Denver, Colorado.

I went to college in Memphis, TN, where I got a degree in marketing. This has been widely regarded as a bad idea. With that being said, I did some soul searching and decided to enter the world of web.

I began my world of web as a web designer, but have since moved into the entire stack of web development. I love all things web, but will always have a deep love for the front-end side of development.

I am proficient with the following technologies: HTML5, CSS, SASS, javascript including jQuery, Angular, node.js. Databses I have worked with include mySQL, Postgres, and have worked with noSQL databases such as mongo

The world is always changing and IT is no exception. I love keeping my ear to the ground with regards to new technology. I really do spend weekends brushing up on a new technology. I design for cross-browser compatibility, and design with mobile first in mind. We all know if a site doesn't look good a person will leave and I pay close attention to make everything modern and sleek.
